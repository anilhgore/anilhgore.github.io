Anil H. Gore

Associate Professor (Analytical Chemistry)

Dr. Anil H. Gore is an Associate Professor of Analytical Chemistry at Tarsadia Institute of Chemical Science, UKA Tarsadia University, Surat, Gujarat, India. His research focuses on micro/nanoplastics analysis, sustainable nanotechnology, carbonaceous nanomaterials, and environmental remediation. He has 70+ publications, 4 patents, h-index 28, and 2660+ citations.
