Dr. Anil H. Gore was invited as a **Resource Person and Guest Speaker** at the **Department of Physics, Khalifa University of Science and Technology**, Abu Dhabi, UAE on **19th February 2025**.

## Lecture Topic

**"Micro and Nano Plastics: Global Threats, Analysis and Premeditation Challenges"**

The talk covered:

- The growing global crisis of micro and nanoplastic pollution
- Current analytical challenges in detection, separation, and quantification
- State-of-the-art methodologies for microplastic extraction from complex matrices
- Health risk assessment frameworks and regulatory perspectives
- Our lab's contributions to developing green miniaturized analytical methods

## International Visibility

This invited lecture at a world-ranked UAE university reflects the growing international recognition of our research group's work on microplastics analysis. Our collaborative network now spans institutions across **South Korea** (Korea University, Konkuk University, Hanyang University, Gachon University), **Japan** (Mie University), and **UAE** (Khalifa University).
