Our latest research on **Nanocarbon Eco-Hydrogel Kit** for environmental monitoring has been published in *Analyst* (RSC, Impact Factor: 3.6), a leading journal in analytical science.

## Innovation Highlights

This work introduces a portable, on-site nanocarbon eco-hydrogel kit capable of:

- **Visual metal ion sensing** — rapid, naked-eye detection of heavy metal contaminants in water samples
- **Dye cleanup** — efficient removal of hazardous organic dyes from contaminated water
- **Circular economy approach** — utilizing waste-derived carbonaceous materials as functional components

## The Circular Economy Vision

The eco-hydrogel kit represents our lab's commitment to **zero waste** and **circular economy** principles. By transforming agricultural and industrial waste materials into high-performance analytical and remediation tools, we demonstrate that sustainable chemistry can be both scientifically rigorous and practically impactful.
